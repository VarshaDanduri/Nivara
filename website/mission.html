Safety First

Every product recommendation is based on scientific research and pregnancy safety guidelines. We avoid ingredients with high systemic absorption rates like hydroquinone and controversial compounds like tretinoin.

Holistic Wellness

We understand that pregnancy brings hormonal changes that can affect your skin. Our approach addresses both the physical and emotional aspects of skincare during this transformative time.

Why Nirvara Exists

Many women feel deeply connected to their skincare routine as a form of self-care and self-expression. When pregnancy brings about significant hormonal changes, this connection can feel disrupted or complicated by safety concerns.

We created Nirvara because we believe that every woman deserves to feel beautiful and connected to herself throughout her pregnancy journey. Our app serves as a trusted companion, providing safe, scientifically-backed skincare guidance that allows you to continue nurturing yourself while protecting your growing baby.

Through careful research and expert guidance, we've curated a selection of pregnancy-safe ingredients and products that act locally with minimal systemic absorption, ensuring both your safety and your continued self-care routine.
