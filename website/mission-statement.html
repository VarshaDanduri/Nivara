Mission Statement

Our commitment to pregnant women everywhere

Our Mission Statement

"To empower pregnant women to maintain their skincare routines as a form of self-love and self-care, while ensuring the safety of both mother and child through scientifically-backed, pregnancy-friendly products and guidance."

Our Core Principles

01

Safety Above All

We prioritize the health and safety of both mother and child. Every recommendation is based on peer-reviewed research and pregnancy safety guidelines, avoiding ingredients with high systemic absorption rates.

02

Self-Care as Self-Love

We believe that skincare is a powerful form of self-care and self-expression. During pregnancy, maintaining this connection to oneself becomes even more important as women navigate significant physical and emotional changes.

03

Scientific Integrity

Our recommendations are grounded in scientific evidence. We reference studies on topical agent absorption, pregnancy safety data, and dermatological research to ensure our guidance is both safe and effective.

04

Empowerment Through Knowledge

We provide women with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed decisions about their skincare during pregnancy, empowering them to feel confident and beautiful throughout their journey.

Our Research Foundation

Our mission is supported by extensive research on pregnancy-safe skincare ingredients. Based on studies published in peer-reviewed journals, we know that:

  • Benzoyl peroxide is not detected in serum and produces minimal systemic levels
  • Salicylic acid is undetectable in normal skin when used in appropriate concentrations
  • Glycolic acid has limited absorption and is considered safe for topical use
  • Hydroquinone has high systemic absorption (35-45%) and should be avoided
  • Tretinoin has controversial evidence and should be used with extreme caution

Source: Safety of skin care products during pregnancy - Canadian Family Physician, 2011
